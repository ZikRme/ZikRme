## What is ZikRme?

<table>
<tr>
<td width="60%" valign="top">

**ZikRme** is India's all-in-one smart travel & lifestyle platform — built for every kind of traveler, from the solo backpacker to the family planner to the business traveler.

We're bringing together everything you need for a journey — **hotels, cabs, restaurants, tour packages, adventure activities, pilgrimage routes, workspaces and local experiences** — under a single intelligent platform, powered by technology and built for Bharat.

<br/>

> *"To make every journey seamless — from the first search to the last mile — for every Indian traveler, wherever the road takes them."*

<br/>

⚡ Menu card upload &nbsp;&nbsp;·&nbsp;&nbsp; ⚡ Cuisine & specialty tagging &nbsp;&nbsp;·&nbsp;&nbsp; ⚡ Seating capacity & hours &nbsp;&nbsp;·&nbsp;&nbsp; ⚡ Event & birthday hosting flag

<summary><b>&nbsp;Cab Services · Car & Bike Rentals</b></summary>
<br/>

List your fleet and connect with intercity and local travelers. Whether it's chauffeur-driven, self-drive cars, or bike rentals — all accepted.

⚡ Fleet types & vehicle inventory &nbsp;&nbsp;·&nbsp;&nbsp; ⚡ Per-km or fixed rate pricing &nbsp;&nbsp;·&nbsp;&nbsp; ⚡ Driver verification &nbsp;&nbsp;·&nbsp;&nbsp; ⚡ Fuel & security deposit terms

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>&nbsp;Travel Agencies · Tour Packages · Guide Services</b></summary>
<br/>

Showcase fixed group tours, custom itineraries, MICE packages or local guide services to travelers looking to plan their next journey.

⚡ Package inclusions builder &nbsp;&nbsp;·&nbsp;&nbsp; ⚡ Advance booking policy &nbsp;&nbsp;·&nbsp;&nbsp; ⚡ Cancellation & refund matrix &nbsp;&nbsp;·&nbsp;&nbsp; ⚡ Guide language proficiency tags
